Natural Pest Control

Natural pest control is an eco-friendly method of managing pests using natural predators, beneficial insects, and plant-based solutions instead of chemical pesticides. Techniques include introducing ladybugs to eat aphids, using neem oil or garlic spray to repel harmful insects, and planting companion crops that deter pests. This approach helps maintain a healthy balance in the ecosystem, protects beneficial organisms, and keeps the soil and crops safe from toxic residues. It's a sustainable and safe way for farmers to protect their crops.
