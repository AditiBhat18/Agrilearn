Rainwater Harvesting

Rainwater harvesting is the practice of collecting and storing rainwater for agricultural use. Agriculturists can capture rainwater from rooftops, fields, or specially designed catchment areas and store it in tanks, ponds, or underground reservoirs. This stored water can be used for irrigation during dry periods, reducing dependence on groundwater and improving water availability. Rainwater harvesting helps conserve water, recharge groundwater levels, and supports sustainable farming, especially in regions facing water scarcity.
